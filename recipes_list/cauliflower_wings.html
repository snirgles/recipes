Cauliflower Wings!

Buffalo Cauliflower Wings

This recipe uses tortilla chips which adds a new level of crunch!

Ingredients

  • 1 head of cauliflower, rinsed and cut into pieces OR a pre-cut bag of cauliflower
  • 2/3 cup flour
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/4 tsp pepper
  • 2/3 cup + a splash of milk of choice
  • Tortilla chips
  • 1/4-1/2 cup buffalo sauce

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.
  2. Cut the cauliflower into bite-sized pieces.
  3. Combine flour and spices in a bowl.
  4. Add the milk and stir until combined. The mixture should still be pretty thick, NOT very runny.
  5. Put the cauliflower pieces into the batter and coat the pieces evenly.
  6. Crush up the tortilla chips in a bowl or on a plate and coat each piece.
  7. Spread pieces evenly on a greased baking sheet (to avoid using extra oil, you can use parchment paper).
  8. Bake at 400 for 20 minutes.
  9. Put the buffalo sauce into a bowl. Coat the pieces in the sauce and then spread evenly on the baking sheet again.
  10. Bake at 400 for 25 minutes.
